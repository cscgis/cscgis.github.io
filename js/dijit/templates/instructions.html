In addition to the mouse you may:

  • Use Tab key to navigate from item to item.
  • Use SHIFT Tab to navigate backwards.
  • Hit SPACE bar to toggle a focused check box.
  • Press ENTER to activate the focused item.
  • Press Alt + Arrow Down keys to expand a combo box.
  • Use Alt with a digit for navigate fast - hint will display.
    (On iOS, the Alt key is the combination Ctrl + Option keys.)
  • Use ESC to go to the parent of the focus element.
